How It Works

Step 1: Provide Inputs

Enter demographic and academic-related fields such as gender, race/ethnicity, parental education, lunch type, and test preparation status.

Step 2: Data Processing

Inputs are cleaned and converted using one-hot encoding ensuring the model receives structured numeric data.

Step 3: ML Prediction

The trained regression model maps user inputs to learned dataset patterns and predicts Maths, Reading, and Writing scores.

Step 4: Results & Insights

The system displays predicted scores and offers further visualization insights for analysis.
